Symposium Theme

"Symposium on Global Research Collaboration of SAARC Nations in AI and Data Science"

The Symposium for SAARC Nations Participants is to provide a vibrant platform for global student researchers to showcase their innovative work, share cultural and academic experiences, and build international collaborations in the rapidly advancing domains of Artificial Intelligence and Data Sciences.

This symposium will facilitate interaction among students, young researchers, and academicians from SAARC Nations. It will emphasize how global perspectives and diverse academic backgrounds contribute to breakthroughs in Image Processing, Data Sciences, Artificial Intelligence and other related technologies.
